Our Story

AndesGold is a premier exporter of Hass avocados grown in the fertile Colombian Andes. Our orchards benefit from nutrient‑rich soils, abundant water and ideal climates, producing fruit with the creamy texture and rich flavor demanded by discerning consumers.

Colombia devotes over 35,000 hectares to Hass avocado production and exported more than 112,000 tons between January and October 2024, generating USD 248 million and ranking among the world’s top suppliers. By partnering with us, you tap into a reliable source of sustainable premium fruit.

Our Hass Avocados

  • Unmatched Quality: Carefully harvested at peak ripeness for creamy texture and rich flavor.
  • Sustainable Cultivation: Eco‑friendly farming practices that protect soils, water and biodiversity.
  • Global Accessibility: Thanks to Colombia’s trade agreements, our avocados reach over 30 countries.

Global Shipping

Our logistics experts coordinate every step from orchard to destination. We provide custom packaging, cold‑chain monitoring and real‑time tracking to ensure your avocados arrive fresh and on time.

We ship year‑round to North America, Europe, Asia and the Middle East. Whether you need a few pallets or full container loads, AndesGold is your partner for dependable supply.

Giving Back

Environmental Stewardship

Colombia is home to vast forests threatened by deforestation. We donate a portion of our profits to projects that plant trees, restore habitats and promote deforestation‑free rural development.

Agricultural Education

Persistent gaps in rural education limit opportunities for young Colombians. Our programs provide farmers and students with training in sustainable agriculture, business skills and innovation, empowering the next generation of growers.

Community Health

Remote communities often travel hours to reach basic health care. We support mobile clinics and health initiatives that improve access to medical services for women, children and vulnerable populations.
